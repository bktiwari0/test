Hotel PMS - Smart Hotel Management

Insynchospitality PMS is designed to help hoteliers manage their everyday operations smartly with detailed analytics and reporting. Automate and simplify everyday tasks such as reservations, check-ins/outs, invoicing and billing, maintenance services, etc. with our Hotel PMS.

  • Faster check-in/out process for hotels.
  • Simplified management of hotel reservations.
  • Smart and effortless rate management.
  • Cloud-based Hotel PMS to manage operations on-the-go.
  • Manage Housekeeping, Laundry, etc. from Hotel PMS.
  • Integrated Customer Feedback System to enhance performance.

Hotel Booking Engine

With our Booking Engine, hoteliers can empower their hotel websites to capture and process direct online guest reservations. Avoid paying high commissions to third-party OTAs and enable direct bookings by embedding the Booking application powered by Insynchospitality Booking Engine.

  • Faster check-in/out process for hotels.
  • Simplified management of hotel reservations.
  • Smart and effortless rate management.
  • Cloud-based Hotel PMS to manage operations on-the-go.
  • Manage Housekeeping, Laundry, etc. from Hotel PMS.
  • Integrated Customer Feedback System to enhance performance.
